Our Story

"We're having a halo party at Ryans this weekend. You can arrive around 7:00 p.m." This was told to all the swimmers after practice one day in early October back in 2010. Everyone who had an xbox brought one the day of the party. Now when you think of a highschool party, what do people normally think? Drinking? Sex? Loud music? Well, for us swimmers, it was all about gaming. and snacks. Specifically flaming hot cheetos, moutain dew, and, of course, LITTLE DEBBIE SNACK CAKES! The environment was very different from what you'd normally expect, but we embraced the situation and made jokes and later, we decided to mess around and say we were an esports team, Little Debbie.

We mostly made Debbie Gaming as a gag joke, but decided to just enter into local competitions for shits and giggles. We liked to pretend to be a professional esports team; it was fun to entertain the idea. We did Microsoft store competitions for Fortnite, Halo, and Rocket League. We even competed in some online games as well, like Call Of Duty.

Debbie Gaming really became more than a gag joke and more of an association to a group of friends. This happened all from one tournament: The Halo 2 World Championships. Now remember, we were swimmers and spent a lot of our time swimming and doing school. We loved to play Halo, we certainly weren't professional level. We had a lot of swimmers who wanted to experience the championship. Anyone could join if they had a team name and gamertags. So, we had 2 teams for Debbie Gaming. I think that's what screwed us to be honest. 64 teams or so entered for this tournament. The people organizing the bracket must've thought we were some unheard of legends or something because our best team had their first match against one of the top 8 teams competing for the grand $50,000 prize. Team slayer: 4-50, Capture the flag: 0-3, Bombs: 0-3. We got recked hard. Needless to say, the whole experience was fun and engaging and ultimately brought all of us swimmers together past just team mates, but good friends as well. That's our story in a nutshell!

Feel free to explore our website, check out our latest reviews and videos, and don't hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions or suggestions. Happy gaming!
